Abercrombie & Scents – Handcrafted Goat Milk Soaps

At Abercrombie & Scents, we craft small-batch, goat milk soaps designed to nourish your skin and awaken your senses. Each bar is made with love, using natural ingredients and simple, soothing formulas that are perfect for daily self-care or thoughtful gifting.

Our soaps are featured weekly during The V-Shop's Thursday livestreams, where you can discover new scents, seasonal collections, and custom bundles. Whether you're buying for yourself or someone you love, you're getting more than a soap—you're getting a handcrafted experience.

  • Gentle, skin-loving ingredients like goat milk and essential oils

  • One-of-a-kind scents and seasonal soap collections

  • Sold exclusively through live shows or direct message

Are your soaps safe for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our soaps are made with goat milk and natural ingredients that are gentle on all skin types—including sensitive skin. We avoid harsh chemicals, parabens, and artificial dyes.

How do I order soaps if I miss the live?

You can always message us directly on Facebook or Instagram to ask about what's still available from the most recent live. We’ll confirm the scents in stock and help you place your order.

How long does a bar of soap last?

Our handcrafted bars typically last 3–4 weeks with regular use. To extend their life, keep them in a dry, well-drained soap dish between uses.

Do you make custom gift sets?

Yes! We love creating custom soap bundles for birthdays, holidays, baby showers, or just because. You can request specific scents, packaging, and even pairing with boutique items from The V-Shop.

What makes goat milk soap special?

Goat milk is rich in vitamins, minerals, and moisturizing fats that help nourish and hydrate the skin. It’s a natural cleanser that gently exfoliates without drying out your skin—perfect for daily use.
