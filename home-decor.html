Home Décor – Styled with Heart, Chosen with Care

At Smiling Spaces, we believe your home should reflect your personality and bring joy to your daily life. Our home décor collection features custom-made and curated items like wine glasses, vases, statement plates, and more—each designed to elevate your space with intention and beauty.

All décor is featured during The V-Shop’s Thursday night livestreams, where we showcase one-of-a-kind pieces and limited quantities you won’t find anywhere else. Whether you’re decorating a space or searching for a heartfelt gift, these pieces are made to stand out.

  • Unique, limited-run pieces that add charm to any space

  • Handcrafted and curated with love and intention

  • Available exclusively through our weekly live shows

How do I order home décor items?

You can shop our home décor items during The V-Shop’s Thursday night live shows. Just comment the item number during the live and send us a message—we’ll follow up to confirm your order and arrange shipping.

How do I order soaps if I miss the live?

You can always message us directly on Facebook or Instagram to ask about what's still available from the most recent live. We’ll confirm the scents in stock and help you place your order.

Are the décor items one-of-a-kind?

Yes! Most of our décor pieces are handcrafted or limited in quantity. Once they’re gone, they’re gone—so be sure to tune in early to claim your favorites.

What types of items do you sell?

We feature a rotating collection of unique home accents like custom wine glasses, ceramic vases, serving trays, statement plates, and more—each designed to add beauty and personality to your space.

Do you offer gift wrapping or bundles?

Yes! We offer custom wrapping for décor items and can create gift bundles that pair beautifully with products from The V-Shop or Abercrombie & Scents.

Can I request a custom item?

Depending on the item and availability, yes. You can message us with custom décor requests and we’ll let you know what’s possible!
