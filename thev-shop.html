The V-Shop: Boutique Style Meets Handmade Charm

At The V-Shop, we believe in making shopping feel personal, joyful, and one-of-a-kind. Every Thursday night from 7–8:30 PM CST, we go live on Facebook and Instagram to showcase new arrivals—from curated fashion and jewelry to elegant home décor like custom wine glasses, vases, and more.

Each item is limited, often one-of-a-kind, and packed with love. To claim an item during the live, simply comment the number shown, send a message, and we’ll get it shipped to your doorstep. We’re not just selling items—we’re sharing pieces that help you express your style and brighten your space.

  • Weekly live shows every Thursday at 7PM to 8:30PM CST

  • Unique clothing, accessories & handmade home décor

  • Simple claim-and-message system with direct shipping

How do I shop during the live?

Join us on Facebook or Instagram every Thursday at 7PM CST! When you see something you love, simply comment the item number shown on screen and send us a message to confirm. That’s it—we’ll take care of the rest..

How do I pay for my items?

Currently, we’ll message you directly after the live to confirm your total and arrange secure payment. We’re working on adding a payment option directly through the website soon!

What kinds of items do you sell?

We offer stylish women’s clothing, statement jewelry, and custom home décor like wine glasses, vases, and trays. Every piece is personally curated or handmade and usually one-of-a-kind!

Can I shop if I miss the live?

Yes! After each live, we post a recap of available items. You can message us to check what’s still in stock and claim anything you missed.

Do you offer shipping?

Absolutely! Once you’ve claimed your item(s), we’ll arrange shipping so they’re delivered straight to your door.
